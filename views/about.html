Course Description

The purpose of this course is to provide an overview of quantitative trading and help students develop the skills necessary to solve problems in financial markets. The course places a strong emphasis on quantitative approaches to trading, but is designed to be accessible to students of various academic backgrounds. After taking this course, students will understand how to become further involved in quantitative trading (either independently or by joining a trading firm).
